About the Project

Waddle Island Adventures is an indie game being developed by me, a solo developer passionate about creating fun and immersive experiences. You play as a brave green duck who must save the world from an evil frog by gliding through lush forests, treacherous swamps, and mysterious caves.

This dev log will document my progress, challenges, and milestones as I bring the game slowly to life. Stay tuned for regular updates every month!
